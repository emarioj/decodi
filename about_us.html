Who we are

Christ being the center of our implementation, we are supplementing efforts by government and other organisations within our scope of work with the aim of achieving sustainable growth and development at household level. We celebrate the milestone achieved so far, through the existing functional community structures, engagements with local government, local churches, international friends, advocates and donors who have walked the path with us in the journey, to bring about lasting solutions.

We begun our journey in 2007, with volunteers who mobilized women groups and formed Village Savings Associations (VSLA), giving birth to our first initiatives and later, the women’s conference that we have continually held. Today we celebrate the exitance of a SACCO born of the small associations.

Under Spiritual Empowerment, the conferences gather all our beneficiaries to share testimonies but also pray together and follow-ups are made through the subcounty Alters under the Missions department. And because we believe that these hurting women are strong, brave, smart and beautiful, our conferences seek to plant seeds of faith that ignite the women to seek restoration for the lost honor and glory that God has placed in them.

Surely the Lord is restoring His people. So far, our journey has impacted 8,709 women households (Single women and widows), an average of 150,000 beneficiaries, who are now confident in the fact that; they are loved and Worth Loving!
